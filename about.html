A bit about me

A creative traveller

My name is Sarah Korsgaard, and I am currently studying multimedia design at UCL Seebladsgade in Odense, Denmark. Although I am at the beginning of my education, I have long been passionate about pursuing different creative outlets. My interests include content creation, design, prop-making, and visual art, allowing me to explore creativity in many forms. Alongside my creative interests, I have always been deeply curious about the world. Growing up, I was fortunate to travel to many different places, which sparked a lasting fascination with different cultures, environments, and possible experiences. By combining my love for travel with my creative skills, I aim to create engaging, aesthetic, and meaningful content that inspires curiosity in others. My goal is to help brands build a strong and eye-catching presence both online and offline, encouraging people to explore and experience the world with the help of your company.
